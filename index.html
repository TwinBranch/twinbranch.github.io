Raspberry Pi Song

The goal of the project is to recreate a well known song with a raspberry pi via the "clicks" it makes when coded in Python. To ensure success, the song will be well-known, and will be easy to manipulate the clicks so it goes along with the beat on the song. The largest challenge facing this project is getting the clicks at the exact tenth of a second, as doing that could possibly disrupt the rest of the song.
